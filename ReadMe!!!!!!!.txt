About:
------
This is a Video Library with options to sort \ filter \ and type your favorite movie to see the full description.
you can also navigate back and forward in order to see all other movies in the store.


How to Navigate:
----------------
1. Online webApp : http://roni.pe.hu/cinemabox/
2. Gitlab link : https://gitlab.com/Roni691986/video-library
3. Git: https://gitlab.com/Roni691986/video-library.git (you can download by pulling into your favorite IDE).
